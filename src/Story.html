CirQle is a community for quantum computing enthusiasts to collaborate, learn and teach together. The inception was at Google DevFest19 Kochi, when an idea struck our founders Augustin and Kiran, and they decided to lay the foundations for a creative space for people already in the quantum computing industry and newcomers alike. Just like that, CirQle was born! Today, CirQle has a team of quantum researchers, scientists and engineers from around the world, actively involved in imparting knowledge and developing skills.

As we approach physical limits on human technology, it is only sensible to turn to quantum phenomena to aid us in computation. We realise this, and it is this knowledge that we seek to spread. Besides, we love everything quantum!
